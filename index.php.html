Questions and Answers

1. What do I need to play?

You must download the chrome tronlink extension. After that, purchase Tron (TRX) with BTC on binance. You may then start investing your tron in our smart contract games.

2. How much will I earn?

You will earn a base rate of 3.50% profit daily. Your tier will be added to the base rate, if you invest a larger amount of tron.

3. How are funds distributed?

7% affiliate (if any) and 5% dev commision. The rest of the funds go to investors.

4. What if the site disappears?

Nothing! The contract will forever run on the Tron blockchain. The site is simply a UI to interect with the contract.
